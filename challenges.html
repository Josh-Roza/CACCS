Challenges

-Teachers don't always tell you when you have assignments to do.

-I learned this the hard way.

-I had trouble staying on top of all my assignments.

-Things can stack up on you in college, make sure you keep a calendar.

-Some classes base their grade off of 4 or fewer tests. That can create a lot of pressure.

-Make sure you find and prepare for those tests, I learned that from experience.

-It has been difficult to find an internship. Companies don't often hire freshmen.

-I will get some of my coding projects on the resume so I can demonstrate my coding prowess.
 Also, I will stay persistent and keep applying on Intern NE. Intern Nebraska is a program we
 learned about in class. It gives companies grant money if they take interns.
